Introduction to IGP SmartForce Database Management Centre

About IGP SmartForce Database Management Centre

The IGP’s-SmartForce and Database Management Unit is an initiative of the present Administration of the Nigeria Police Force aimed at integrating the Force into ICT-Base System to simplify the management of its personnel records. It is designed as a cutting edge IT solution for managing the entire workforce of the Nigeria Police Force. The System uniquely manages/identifies each personnel of the Force. The system is structured with modern technological concepts to monitor the distribution of personnel workforce and access across the country.

IGP SmartForce Database Management Centre

What To Expect From IGP SmartForce

Purpose
The GP SmartForce Data Management section in the office of the Force Secretary is integrated with modern database technology to ensure effective and efficient management of records  of all personnel of the Nigeria Police Force. 
Tracking
The Centre will aid the office of the Force Secretary in planning for deployment of personnel in the Force, budgetary related issues, tracing/identification of Police personnel, retrieval of records as it relates to serving and even retired Police personnel.
User Friendly
A user friendly platform that can capture and decipher all information regarding any member of the Force in realtime giving accurate number of all personnel of the Force.
Records
IGP SmartForce will aid ease of updating records and guarantee personnel information security as well as the credibility of the database.
Hi Tech
A modernized hi-tech biometric machine with unique features like Fingerprint scanner, 2D Scanner, Face Recognized RJ45 PORT+BIOS.
SmartForce ID Card
SmartForce ID Card, an important component of SmartForce office, plays a key role in integration of data of all officers in the Force.
Up To Date
IGP’s SmartForce and Database Centre constantly updates the database and feed the Department of ICT with quarterly, month and/ or weekly returns for their Force personnel records back-up.
Storage
The SmartForce Storage Servers are domiciled in C4i facilities and SmartForce office in the Force Headquarters, Abuja and managed by Police Officers with proven integrity and many years of experience in Database Technology.
